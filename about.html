About Me

I'm a UX designer with a passion for creating meaningful experiences that blend creativity with functionality. My journey in design is complemented by my love for drawing, which helps me approach problems with a unique visual perspective.

When I'm not designing interfaces, you'll find me sketching, exploring new art techniques, or experimenting with different mediums to express ideas visually.

My Design Journey

My path to UX design began with a fascination for how people interact with technology. I've always been curious about the psychology behind user behavior and how design can influence experiences in meaningful ways.

With a background that blends technical understanding with creative expression, I approach each project with empathy and attention to detail. I believe that great design should not only look beautiful but also feel intuitive and solve real problems.

My drawing practice informs my design work by training my eye for composition, balance, and visual storytelling. Whether I'm sketching user flows or creating illustrations, I'm always thinking about how to communicate ideas clearly and effectively.
